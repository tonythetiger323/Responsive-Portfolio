About Me

Tony Greeley was born March 23, 1982 at Central DuPage Hospital in Winfield, IL. By 1st grade his family moved to Wheaton, IL where he grew up, graduating from Wheaton Warrenville South High School in June of 2000. Tony went on to obtain a diploma and ministry certificate from Omaha Master's Commission in Omaha, NE. After that he obtained an Associates in Business Administration from Colorado Technical University Online.


Tony is currenlty a Genius (aka Apple Certifided Mac Technician) at Apple Lincoln Park and has worked there for 8 years as of July 12th, 2018. Tony spent 2 of those years working in sales and the remaing time has been in tech support. Tony is currenlty enrolled in a part time full stack coding bootcamp at the Northwestern University School of Professional Studies.


In additon to being a bit of a tech nerd, Tony is also a big Chicago Cubs and Blackhawks fan, and loves all things of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (proud member of Team Cap). Tony is also a proud Uncle and has 2 nephews, Luke (8) and Noah (6), and a niece, Fatih (5).
